Over the last 13 years, the project has produced **two tool suites** (**ODK**, **ODK-X**), and in the process, has become home to **a large community of users, implementers and developers**.

To continue to build the data collection community that has grown with the tool suites, we are retiring the use of the “Open Data Kit” brand and forming a new umbrella organization, The Data Software for Social Good Initiative.

The new initiative aims to expand collaboration among the data-driven tools for social good ecosystem, and welcome a diverse set of member projects.
